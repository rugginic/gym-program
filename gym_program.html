PROGRESSION PLAN

Phase Focus Action Items
Weeks 1-2 Form & Consistency - Focus on proper technique
- Use moderate weights
- Complete all prescribed reps
Weeks 3-4 Progressive Overload - Increase weights by 5-10%
- Add 1-2 reps if weights feel too easy
- Maintain strict form
Weeks 5-8 Intensity & Volume - Further increase weights
- Add an additional set to primary exercises
- Reduce rest times by 15 seconds

SUCCESS TIPS

  • Track your weights and reps in a fitness app to monitor progress
  • Prioritize proper form over lifting heavier weights
  • Stay hydrated throughout your workout (bring a water bottle)
  • Get a quick form check from gym staff if unsure about technique
  • On very busy days, focus on completing just the first two exercises
  • Take progress photos every 2 weeks (same position, lighting, time of day)
  • Ensure adequate protein intake (1.6-2g per kg bodyweight)
  • Get 7-8 hours of quality sleep for optimal recovery

Important: Listen to your body. If you feel pain (not normal muscle fatigue), stop the exercise immediately. Proper form is more important than weight or reps.

Back Pain Management Tips

  • Always engage your core to stabilize your spine during exercises
  • Use machines with back support whenever possible
  • Consider wearing a lightweight supportive belt for certain exercises
  • Apply heat before workouts to loosen muscles (if recommended by your doctor)
  • Apply ice after workouts if you experience any discomfort
  • Maintain proper posture throughout your day, not just during exercise
  • Consider consulting with a physical therapist to personalize this program further
