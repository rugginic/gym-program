MONDAY: LOWER BODY + CORE

Warm-up (5 min)

  • March in place: 1 min
  • Leg swings: 10 each direction
  • Bodyweight squats: 10 reps

Main Workout (35 min)

Exercise Sets × Reps Rest
Leg Press [Video] 4 × 12,10,8,8 60s [Start]
Romanian Deadlifts [Video] 3 × 12,10,10 60s [Start]
Seated Leg Curls [Video] 3 × 12 60s [Start]
Standing Calf Raises [Video] 3 × 15 60s [Start]

Core Circuit (5 min) - Back Support

  • Plank (on knees if needed): 30s hold
  • Bird-dog: 10 each side
  • Dead bug: 10 each side
  • Rest 30s, repeat 2 rounds

Focus: This program is designed for back health and fat loss. Lower body strength training combined with core stability work helps protect your spine while building muscle and burning calories.

Back Safety: Always maintain a neutral spine position. If you feel any pain (not just muscle fatigue) in your back, stop immediately and consult with a physical therapist.

WEDNESDAY: BACK & CORE FOCUS

Warm-up (5 min)

  • Arm circles: 10 each direction
  • Cat-cow stretch: 10 reps
  • Band pull-aparts: 15 reps

Main Workout (35 min)

Exercise Sets × Reps Rest
Lat Pulldowns [Video] 4 × 12,10,8,8 60s [Start]
Seated Cable Rows [Video] 4 × 12,10,10,10 60s [Start]
Face Pulls [Video] 3 × 15 60s [Start]
Chest Supported Rows [Video] 3 × 12 60s [Start]

Core Circuit (5 min) - Anti-Rotation

  • Pallof Press: 10 each side
  • Side Plank: 20s each side
  • Suitcase Carry: 30s each side (hold dumbbell, walk)
  • Rest 30s, repeat 2 rounds

Focus: Back-focused training is crucial for posture, spinal health, and fat loss. This workout strengthens the entire posterior chain while building core stability through anti-rotation exercises.

FRIDAY: FULL BODY + CARDIO

Warm-up (5 min)

  • March in place: 1 min
  • Arm circles: 10 each direction
  • Bodyweight squats: 10 reps

Main Workout (30 min)

Exercise Sets × Reps Rest
Goblet Squats [Video] 3 × 12 60s [Start]
Dumbbell Chest Press [Video] 3 × 12,10,10 60s [Start]
Single-Arm Dumbbell Row [Video] 3 × 12 each 60s [Start]
Glute Bridges [Video] 3 × 15 60s [Start]

Cardio (5 min)

  • Recumbent bike: 5 min moderate pace

Core Finisher (5 min)

  • Plank: 30s
  • Mountain climbers (slow): 20 total
  • Superman holds: 15s hold × 3
  • Rest 30s between exercises

Focus: This full body workout combines strength training with cardio for maximum fat loss and metabolic benefit. Lower volume allows for proper recovery before starting the next week.

Back Protection: Always maintain proper back support when doing seated exercises. Use the back support on machines and avoid slouching.
